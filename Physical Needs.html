The Physical Needs of an Australian Shepherd

The physical needs of an australian shepherd are very intensive and need to be recognized or else this breed may not be right for you.
Since the Australian Shepherd is a working dog that need over 2 hours of exercise on a daily basis to stay healthy mentally and physically.
If you're unable to exercise these for 2 hours a day because of time restraints, many of them will develop destructive behaviors and attitudes.
The physical needs can be met through a job, intensive outdoor exercise, fetch, agility, trick training, etc.. This will help give the dog something to do, drain energy, and make them well mannered.
Overall, knowing the energy needs of an Australian Shepherd is extremely important because owners who have not done proper research on this aspect, often times depart ways with their dogs.
