Australian Shepherd Demeanor

There can be many reasons why you're on this website learning more information about Australian Shepherds.
At the end of the day whether you're interested in one for work, for comfort, a family dog, or any other need, you can definitley bet that you're going to get a silly and playful dog.
The overall demeanor of an Australian Shepherd is silly, in other words they LOVE to play, whether it's with other dogs or other humans.

Silly
Having or showing a lack of common sense or judgment; absurd and foolish.
Playful
Fond of games and amusement; lighthearted.
