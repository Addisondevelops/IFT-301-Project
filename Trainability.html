Australian Shepherd Trainability

Australian Shepherds are tricky to train at time. In many ways they are very easy dogs to train and very adaptive to their environment and commands.
They can be easily trained by an experienced dog owner who has previous experience with some dog training. Although they are very intelligent they do need to have a patient trainer
There energy and stubborness can make training them a difficult tasks at time. They are very easily distracted by other people and animals.
And there stubborn behavior makes them give up on tricks fairly quickly if the trainer is not strong and confident, as well has the dog's trust.
Overall, we recommend these dogs to not be owned by first time dog owners because of their high energy but they are very trainable with a strong and determined trainer that's patient enough to really utilize their intelligence.
