Australian Shepherd Intelligence

The Australian Shepherd is an extremely intelligent breed of dog. They are natural herding/working dogs that have the mental capacity to learrn thousands of commands and tricks
They are so smart they can hear a command once and learn it, it doesn't take much for the Australian Shepherd to adapt and learn what the trainer is saying.
They are also very emotionally intelligent and can sense someone's energy, whether that's happy, or sad; they will always be there to play or show comfort as times of need.

  • Can Learn Over 1000 Commands
  • Can Actively Perform Multiple Jobs
  • Can Recognize hundreds of people and objects.
